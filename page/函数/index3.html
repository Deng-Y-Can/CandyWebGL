Examples:

  • Sine wave: sin(x) * cos(y)

  • Saddle: x^2 - y^2
  • Gaussian: exp(-(x^2 + y^2) / 4)
  • Ripple: sin(sqrt(x^2 + y^2)) / sqrt(x^2 + y^2 + 0.1)
  • Absolute value pyramid: -(abs(x) + abs(y))
